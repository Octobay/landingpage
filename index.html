Innovative Marketplace to Outsource Tasks

Smart Bounties

Octobay provided trustless bounties for open source projects hosted on GitHub.

Each reward can be stored in a smart contract to act as a middleman between the customer and the developer. Once an issue is closed, the contributor can claim his reward without additional consense from the maintainer.
